1) Tail Asymmetry Score (TAS)

Define TAS in [-1, +1] where:

negative => downside tail dominant (puts are the danger)

positive => upside tail dominant (calls are the danger)

Implement deterministic scoring:

skew_component:

normalize rr25 into [-1,+1] (e.g. clamp(rr25/0.10, -1, +1) assuming rr25 in vol points; adjust if rr25 in percent)

TAS = 0.45history_component + 0.45skew_component + 0.10*regime_component

callWingMultiple = baseWingMultiple * (1 - adj)
