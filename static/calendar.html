SPX reaction stats (how to use)

What it is: historical SPX close→close reaction around this macro event type (matched by Benzinga event dates).

  • Event day: SPX close vs prior trading day close.
  • Next day: follow-through risk (next close vs event-day close).
  • |median|: “typical” magnitude. p90 |abs|: a tail-ish reference.

Desk view: use p90 |abs| to sanity-check wing width / size into the release. If your structure can’t tolerate that move, widen or reduce.

Not a prediction: direction-neutral, not intraday, and doesn’t imply edge.
