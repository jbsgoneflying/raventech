Fasting Guide

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026
Raven Tech respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how Fasting Guide handles information when you use our iOS application.

1. Overview

Fasting Guide is designed to support mindful fasting without collecting, storing, or monetizing personal data. We intentionally built the app to function without requiring user accounts, analytics tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party data sharing.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Fasting Guide does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of the following:

  • Personal identifying information
  • Account credentials
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device fingerprinting data
  • Behavioral or profiling data

We do not operate servers that receive or store user data from the app.

3. Apple Health Integration

Fasting Guide may request permission to access Apple Health data solely to display relevant fasting-related information within the app.

Important points regarding Apple Health data:

  • Apple Health data is accessed only with your explicit permission
  • Data is read locally on your device
  • No Apple Health data is transmitted off your device
  • No Apple Health data is stored by Raven Tech
  • No Apple Health data is shared with third parties
  • No Apple Health data is used for advertising, analytics, or profiling

You may revoke Apple Health permissions at any time through your device settings.

4. How the App Functions

All fasting data, progress indicators, and preferences remain on your device. If you delete the app, all associated data is removed with it.

Fasting Guide does not require account creation or sign-in to function.

5. Third-Party Services

Fasting Guide does not integrate with third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, marketing services, or data brokers.

Apple system services such as Apple Health are governed by Apple's own privacy policies and operate independently of Raven Tech.

6. Children's Privacy

Fasting Guide is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Data Security

Because no personal or health data is collected or stored by Raven Tech, there is no external database or server infrastructure holding user information.

All data you see in the app exists only on your device and is protected by your device's operating system security.

8. Your Choices

You remain in full control of your data at all times:

  • You can enable or disable Apple Health access at any time
  • You can delete the app to remove all local data
  • You are never required to provide personal information

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date above. Any changes will maintain our commitment to minimal data use and user privacy.
