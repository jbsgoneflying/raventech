How Ranking Works
Composite Scoring Methodology

Each ticker is scored on a 0-100 scale based on weighted factors. Higher scores indicate better risk/reward for selling earnings premium.

Note: This does NOT use the binary GO/NO-GO flag. Instead, each underlying factor is scored on a gradient to give you nuanced rankings.

Breach Rate (25%)
Historical frequency of moves exceeding the implied. Lower = better.
IV Elevation (20%)
IV30 percentile rank. Higher IV = richer premium to sell.
EM Richness (15%)
Expected move vs historical median realized. Higher ratio = better cushion.
Liquidity (15%)
Underlying volume + option spread quality. Better liquidity = easier execution.
Tail Coverage (10%)
EM coverage of P90 realized move. Protection against fat tails.
Market Regime (10%)
SPX gamma, RV acceleration, forced flows. Macro backdrop quality.
