Frequently Asked Questions

From the main screen, tap the "Start Fast" button. You can select your preferred fasting protocol (12:12, 16:8, 18:6, etc.) and the app will begin tracking your fast immediately. You'll see a live timer showing your progress toward your fasting goal.
Go to Settings > Default Protocol to change your preferred fasting window. You can choose from various protocols including 12:12, 14:10, 16:8, 18:6, 20:4, OMAD (one meal a day), or extended fasts. Your new protocol will apply to your next fast.
Yes! Fasting Guide works fully offline. All your fasting data, timers, and progress are stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed for initial sign-in and syncing data across devices if you choose to use that feature.
Go to Settings > Apple Health to manage your HealthKit permissions. The app can read relevant health data to enhance your fasting insights. All Apple Health data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can enable or disable this at any time.
You can delete your local data by going to Settings > Export Data, where you'll find an option to clear all app data. Alternatively, deleting the app from your device will remove all associated data. If you need help with data deletion, contact us at info@raven-tech.co.
Absolutely. Fasting Guide is designed with privacy first. We don't collect personal data, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers. Your fasting data stays on your device. Apple Health data is accessed only with your permission and never leaves your device. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
