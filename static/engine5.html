Volatility Lead-Lag
Vol Lead-Lag

Detects when global volatility information has not yet been priced into US options. Adjusts strike width, sizing, and structure selection for weekly income trades.

  • UNDERPRICED_RISK: Global vol rising, US IV flat/low — widen strikes
  • OVERPRICED_RISK: Global vol falling, US IV high — vol decay edge
  • CONFIRMED_STRESS: Both rising — risk acknowledged
  • NORMAL: No actionable mismatch

Weekly Trade Ideas

Structured ideas for weekly income harvesting. These are model-generated suggestions, not trade orders.

  • Confidence: Signal strength weighted by regime
