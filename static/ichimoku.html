Market Gamma Context
Market Gamma Context (Segmented by Index)

What is dealer gamma? Options market makers must hedge their positions. When "long gamma," they buy dips and sell rips. When "short gamma," they do the opposite.

For Ichimoku continuation setups:

  • Positive gamma: Supports pullback continuation (dealers buy the dip)
  • Negative gamma: Can accelerate moves - be more selective

S&P 500 names use SPX gamma; Nasdaq 100 names use NDX/QQQ gamma.
